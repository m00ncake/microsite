The Ultimate Guide to SMS Marketing

What is MMS?

MMS stands for Multimedia Messaging Service. It's also commonly referred to as a picture message. MMS are text messages that include images, audio, and video files - unlike a regular SMS text that only includes 160 alpha-numeric characters.

Why Use MMS?

They say a picture's worth a 1000 words, and we agree! Using a well-chosen image can make your text message really stand out. Think of all the sizzle you can convey with a great picture or additional text.

What Works Best

If you're trying to sell something - show it in your text ! We've found product images are especially successful in driving bigger results. But really the sky, and your imagination are the limit.

You Can Do More With MMS

In addition to media files, MMS lets your send text-only messages that are longer than 160 characters. All of these capabilites give you greater flexibility in communicating your message.
