Frequently Asked Questions:

What are Toll-Free Numbers?

Toll-Free Numbers are 10-digit numbers that begin with the prefix 800, 888, 877, 866, 855, 844, or 833. Carriers allow for these numbers to transmit mass text messages for commercial use.

What Does This Mean for the Shared Short Codes?

AT&T has already said that it plans to stop supporting Shared Codes. Other carriers are expected to follow suit. A Toll-Free Number prepares you for a future without Shared Codes, and is yours alone, giving you the affordability of a Shared Short Code with the security of a dedicated number.

Will a Toll-Free Number Work With a Keyword?

Your new Toll-Free Number will work with Keywords to grow your opt-in list just like a Shared Short Code.

Can I Send an MMS Message Using My Toll-Free Number?

Yes! Your new Toll-Free Number allows for picture and video messages to help boost engagement.
