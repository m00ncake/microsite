Ready to see what you can do? If you've already activated your account and logged in, head over to the Send Text Message page. There are three default groups in your account, but they're empty right now. Try sending a message to a single number using the single number field. You'll have an opportunity to preview your message before you send it.

You should receive your message in under a minute. When you do, text back a reply like It works! Head over to your Club Texting Inbox. Whenever anyone replies to your messages, those responses are automatically delivered to your Inbox.

Replies aren't the only way to receive messages from your customers and contacts. Keywords allow people to text a word to our short code to join your list of Contacts. If you want to see how Keywords work, try texting your account's free demo Keyword to 25827 (212121 if you're in Canada). You can find your free, fully functioning demo Keyword on the Keywords page.

Most Club Texting clients use Keywords - and you can rent them individually, or upgrade to a monthly plan that includes free Keywords.

The Contacts page lists all of your contacts, which you can assign to different Groups. Groups allow you to send a message to everyone in the group at once, whether there are 10 people or 10,000 people in the group.

If you already have a list of opted-in contacts, you can Upload Your Contacts and assign them to a group.
