Dear Group Texting Customer,

As we previously communicated to you, Group Texting is making the difficult decision to suspend its service with your company as of May 31, 2021, due growing compliance requirements from carriers and federal regulations. However, we’re excited to announce that we’ve partnered with springbig to service your account starting on June 1, 2021.

Springbig is a leading loyalty, CRM, and text marketing platform. Based in Boca Raton, Florida, springbig’s founders have more than 20 years of experience in creating loyalty rewards programs for retail businesses, and recognized the opportunity to bring advanced loyalty programs and other marketing tools to underserved industries, including gambling businesses.

Here’s what you need to know for a successful transition:

  • Springbig has created a transition webpage with the information required for you to get started on its platform. Your first step is to click the Request A Demo button at the bottom of the page.
  • Ryan Vanderford with springbig will be your primary contact in migrating your business. He can be reached at rvanderford@springbig.com.
  • You should export your contacts from the Group Texting platform before May 31, 2021. This is recommended whether you transition to springbig or another provider.
  • Springbig will honor the remaining credits in your Group Texting account as of May 31, 2021. This process will be automatic and not require any action on your part.
  • If you don’t transition your service to springbig, you will be refunded the amount of your remaining credits as of May 31, 2021. Further communications will follow.

The springbig team is committed to smoothly transitioning your business, and is waiting to hear from you. As always, if you have any questions, please email us at customersupport@grouptexting.com.

Thank you.
